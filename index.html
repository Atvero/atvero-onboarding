Grant Atvero Deployer access to your app catalog

This page grants the Atvero Deployer Entra application FullControl on a single SharePoint site — your app catalog — so Atvero can publish SPFx updates to your tenant automatically. It does not grant access to any other site or data.

Atvero usually pre-fills this for you via the link we sent. Check it's correct before continuing.

Step 1 — Consent to the Atvero Deployer app

Approves the Atvero Deployer application in your tenant so it can authenticate at all. This grants it no data access on its own — step 2 is what actually gives it permission to use your app catalog.

✅ Step 1 complete

Atvero Deployer is now registered in your tenant. One more step: grant it FullControl on just your app catalog site.
